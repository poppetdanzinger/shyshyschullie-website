About

Shy Shy Schullie is a singer and burlesque artist exploring her talent in the lovely city of Montreal. She has been a singer and performer at heart since childhood and began to dabble with many choirs ranging from classical to Broadway.

After spending a few years with the Lyric Theatre Singers, she began to explore the world of jazz by performing at LHotel Montreal, the Wiggle Room, and the Ritz Carlton... and then once she had stepped into the swing dance and jive scene in Montreal, she was propelled into the wonderful world of burlesque. After some experience working with the Montreal International Burlesque Festival and Acme Burlesque, she fell in love with the art.
